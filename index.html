The Overflow Story

Pastor Eddy Nubine, Sr., alongside his wife Patricia and their three children, were inspired to plant a church with a simple goal — to help people connect with God so that they might reach their full potential. Overflow Church began officially on January 28, 2018 at McFee Elementary School in Katy, Texas. From the beginning, the church has focused on reaching those who do not know God personally and building relationships through Small Groups.

Personality / Leadership Style — DISC Assessment

DISC is a simple personality framework that identifies four core behavioral styles: D — Dominant (direct, decisive, driven), I — Influential (inspiring, enthusiastic, people-oriented), S — Steady (stable, loyal, consistent), and C — Compliant (analytical, accurate, detail-oriented). Understanding your DISC style helps you discover how God has uniquely wired you to lead and serve others.

"God has given each of you a gift from His great variety of spiritual gifts. Use them well to serve one another."
— 1 PETER 4:10 NLT
"Leadership is influence. Nothing more. Nothing less."

Leadership is not dependent on titles or positions. Many people hold back due to four common barriers:

"Who am I?"
Insecurity — feeling unqualified
"What if they?"
Fear of others' reactions
"I have never."
Inadequacy / lack of experience
"Use someone else."
Reluctance to step forward
"But you are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that you may proclaim the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvelous light."
— 1 PETER 2:9 NKJV
Qualities of a Leader — Daniel 6:1–3
1
We Love God
Develop your closeness with God, your character, and your calling. "They took note that these men had been with Jesus." — Acts 4:13
2
We Love People
Be a servant. Be a team player. Be real. "Whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant." — Mark 10:43
3
We Pursue Excellence
Do things well. Do them before you're asked. Do more than expected. "He has done everything well." — Mark 7:37
4
We Choose Joy
Be enjoyable. Be positive. Be loyal. "Sorrowful, yet always rejoicing." — 2 Corinthians 6:10
Leader Declaration
Because God has called me to serve my generation, I will value worship over wealth, "we" over "me," character over comfort, service over status, and God's purposes over possessions, positions, popularity, and pleasure.

To my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, I say: However, whenever, wherever, and whatever you ask me to do, my answer in advance is YES! Wherever you lead and whatever the cost, I'm ready anytime, anywhere.

"Well done, good and faithful servant. Come on in, and let the eternal party begin!"
Leadership Honor Code

Overflow Church leaders maintain a disciplined life of Bible reading, prayer, and fasting. As servant-leaders, we commit to refraining from profanity, smoking, gambling, substance abuse, pornography, sexual immorality, and all behaviors which might cause Christ to grieve and others to stumble.

Appendix
Statement of Faith
What We Believe at Overflow Church
Holy Bible
The Holy Bible alone is the authoritative Word of God — inspired, infallible, and inerrant.
2 Timothy 3:16 · 2 Peter 1:20–21 · Proverbs 30:5
Trinity
There is one God, eternally existent in three coequal persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
1 John 5:7 · Matthew 3:16–17 · Matthew 28:19
Jesus Christ
Jesus Christ is God the Son — 100% God and 100% man. Born of a virgin, He died, rose, and will return again.
John 1:1,14 · Isaiah 9:6 · 1 Timothy 2:5
Salvation
We are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ — a gift from God, not works.
Ephesians 2:8–9 · Titus 3:5 · Romans 10:9–10
The Holy Spirit & Spiritual Gifts
The Holy Spirit is manifested through spiritual gifts to build and sanctify the church.
1 Corinthians 12:1–31 · Romans 12:4–8 · 1 Peter 4:10
Baptism
Water baptism follows faith in Jesus Christ. The Lord's Supper remembers His sacrifice.
Matthew 28:19 · Acts 2:38 · 1 Corinthians 11:23–25
Marriage
Marriage is a sacred covenant between one man and one woman before God.
Matthew 19:4–6
Second Coming
Jesus Christ will physically return to establish His Kingdom at an undisclosed time.
Matthew 24:30 · 1 Thessalonians 4:15–17 · Revelation 1:7
